# SEO Keyword Research & Strategy for aaronmolina.me

**Date:** February 3, 2026
**Data Source:** Ahrefs API
**Target Market:** USA (Remote US + Miami)
**Goal:** Attract recruiters, hiring managers, and marketing leadership looking for Jamstack/headless CMS developers

---

## Executive Summary

This research validates and expands upon the initial keyword strategy for aaronmolina.me. The analysis confirms strong opportunities in low-competition "hire" keywords while identifying gaps and new opportunities.

### Key Findings

1. **High-value, low-competition opportunities exist** for "hire jamstack developer" (KD 1), "hire next.js developer" (KD 0), and related terms
2. **Only one individual developer (David Fox)** currently ranks in top 10 for "hire jamstack developer" — clear positioning opportunity
3. **"Hire react developer" has highest volume** (1,000/mo) with low difficulty (KD 8) — should be added to strategy
4. **Agency-style pages dominate SERPs** — individual portfolio positioning can differentiate
5. **"Headless cms developer" is harder** (KD 62) — may require blog content support
